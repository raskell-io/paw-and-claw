# Art Direction

## Visual Identity

**Core Aesthetic**: Charming woodland warfare - serious tactical gameplay wrapped in an inviting, storybook presentation.

**Inspirations**:
- Advance Wars (tactical clarity, readable sprites)
- Redwall (woodland creatures in medieval settings)
- Animal Crossing (warm, inviting color palette)
- Octopath Traveler HD-2D (depth and lighting)

## Style Guidelines

### General

- **Perspective**: Top-down with slight 3/4 view for depth
- **Outlines**: 1px dark outlines for readability
- **Shading**: 2-3 color ramps per material
- **Detail level**: Suggest detail rather than overload

### Characters/Units

- Anthropomorphic animals (bipedal, expressive)
- Species matches faction theme
- Armor/equipment fits medieval-fantasy aesthetic
- Faction colors visible on all units (trim, accents, banners)
- Clear silhouettes even at small sizes

### Terrain

- Natural, organic shapes
- Warm greens and browns dominate
- Buildings feel handcrafted (wood, stone, thatch)
- Water has subtle animation potential (2-3 frames)

### Lighting

- Warm daylight as default
- Light source from upper-left (consistent shadows)
- Weather variants: rain (blue tint), snow (white overlay), fog (desaturated)

## Faction Visual Identity

### Eastern Empire
- **Palette**: Warm reds, dark woods, gold accents
- **Motifs**: Cherry blossoms, curved roofs, paper lanterns
- **Animals**: Tanuki, foxes, red pandas, cranes
- **Armor style**: Samurai-inspired, lacquered

### Northern Realm
- **Palette**: Ice blues, silver, dark fur
- **Motifs**: Snowflakes, pine trees, iron fixtures
- **Animals**: Bears, wolves, owls, elk
- **Armor style**: Viking/Norse, fur-lined

### Western Frontier
- **Palette**: Sage greens, tans, weathered browns
- **Motifs**: Feathers, canyon rock, desert plants
- **Animals**: Raccoons, hawks, coyotes, roadrunners
- **Armor style**: Ranger/scout, leather and cloth

### Southern Pride
- **Palette**: Golden amber, warm browns, sunset oranges
- **Motifs**: Sun symbols, savanna grass, acacia trees
- **Animals**: Lions, elephants, cheetahs, rhinos
- **Armor style**: Regal/ceremonial, gold trim

## Technical Specifications

### Sprite Sizes

| Asset Type | Size | Notes |
|------------|------|-------|
| Terrain tiles | 32x32 | Base ground tiles |
| Terrain features | 32x32 | Trees, rocks, buildings |
| Units | 24x24 | All unit types |
| Unit portraits | 64x64 | CO selection, dialogue |
| UI icons | 16x16 | Buttons, indicators |
| Faction emblems | 32x32 | Flags, markers |

### File Format

- PNG with transparency
- No anti-aliasing on edges (clean pixel art)
- Power-of-2 dimensions preferred for texture atlases

### Animation Frames

- Idle: 2-4 frames
- Move: 4 frames
- Attack: 3-4 frames
- Death: 3 frames
- Water/effects: 3 frames
